Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our courses

Do I need prior programming experience?

No! Our beginner-level courses are designed for complete beginners. We start from the basics and gradually move to advanced concepts. Even if you have no IT background, you can easily learn with our step-by-step approach.

What is the course duration?

Course durations vary depending on the program. Most courses range from 2-4 months. Intensive programs like Python and Java take 3-4 months, while Digital Marketing takes 2 months. We also offer flexible timings with weekend and weekday batches.

What is the fee structure?

Our fees are affordable and vary by course. We offer flexible payment plans and EMI options. Contact us for detailed pricing. We also provide scholarships for deserving candidates.

Will I get job placement support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive placement support including resume building, interview preparation, and direct referrals to our 50+ partner companies. Our 95% placement success rate speaks for itself.

Are classes online or offline?

We offer both offline and online classes based on your convenience. Our offline classes are at our training center in Labbipet, Vijayawada. You can choose the mode that suits your schedule best.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! Upon successful completion of the course, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate. This certificate adds value to your resume and is recognized by leading IT companies.
